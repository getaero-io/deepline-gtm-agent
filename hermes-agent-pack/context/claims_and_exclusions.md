## Directional But Not External Without Review

These are useful internally but require review before external publication:

- customer onboarding reduced from 3-6 weeks to under 5 minutes
- about 100 self-serve customers and 11 enterprise clients in 30 days
- ARR or revenue metrics
- provider-count targets
- coverage percentage claims
- any ROI/pipeline/customer savings numbers not separately verified

## Flagged Claims

Do not use unless Jai explicitly confirms:

- "65% market coverage free"
- "5-7 cents to get to 90%"
- "install in under 2 minutes"
- any generic "80% cheaper than Clay" claim unless framed as Deepline positioning and backed by the exact comparison being made

## Cold Outbound Rule

In cold outbound, the offer should be the free thing:

- repo
- audit
- sample output
- teardown
- useful workflow

The product is what they discover after replying.
